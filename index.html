About DUOS


Dhaka University Optimistic Society is a Social Cultural Student’s Voluntary Organization under TSC, University of Dhaka. This Club is working on Positivity, Happiness and Soul Health issues at the University of Dhaka. Started its journey from 28th November 2018.


Our Vision


Ensuring Soul Peace among the Students of the University of Dhaka.


Motto


Positivity Brings Happiness.
